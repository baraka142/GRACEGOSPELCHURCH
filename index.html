Welcome to Grace Gospel Church

Grace Gospel Church – Webuye is a Christ-centered congregation located in Webuye town, Bungoma County, Kenya. The church operates under the covering of Elshadai Harvest Ministry and exists to reveal Christ Jesus, proclaim His transformative work, and guide believers to live a life rooted in faith.

Our Mission

At Grace gospel Church-Webuye, our mission is to teach,train,equip and send beleivers to live and proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ with power and purpose.We are committed to grounding lives in God's word, nurturing spiritual growth and raising disciples who reveal christ in communities and beyond.Through sound teachin, practical training and spirit-led qequipping,we prepare beleivers to impact society with godly values and love.Our calling is to send out faithfull witnesses who transform lives,restore hope and advance the kingdom of God both locally and globally forHis glory.(PHILEMON 1:11)

Our Vision

From useless to usefulness-revealing Christ, restorinng hope and tranforming lives(LUKE 24:45-49)

Our Slogan

To be committed To know Christ,Yes, To know The Power of His Ressurection, And participation of His suffering, Becoming like Him in Death(PHILIP 3:10)
