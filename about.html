Our Beliefs

Christ-Centered Faith

We believe in the centrality of Jesus Christ in all aspects of faith and life. He is the foundation of our church and the source of our salvation.

Transformation Through God's Word

We believe in the transformative power of Scripture to renew minds and transform lives from uselessness to usefulness for God's kingdom.

Worship, Evangelism & Discipleship

We are committed to authentic worship, passionate evangelism, and intentional discipleship as the core practices of our faith community.

Our Foundational Belief

"We believe in transformation from uselessness to usefulness through the renewal of our minds and being made new. This statement is not merely a slogan; it is a powerful summary of the gospel's practical effect on a believer's life."
